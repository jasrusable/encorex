Investments

Encorex will actively seek to build a diversified portfolio of profitable investments in key economic growth sectors, with a special focus on energy, health and commercial and industrial property. Encorex will welcome partnership opportunities, where such partnerships will help provide demonstrable sustainable win-win benefits to all parties involved. Encorex will prioritise investments that advance safety, health and environmental sustainability. Encorex is a certified as a Level 4 Contributor in terms of the Gazetted Codes of Good Practice of Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment (BEE).
