Godwin Sweto

Encorex was founded in 2013 by Godwin Sweto, an engineer whose 24 years in the minerals and energy industry includes 15 years in oil and gas and 5 years as Vice President for Corporate Strategy and Planning for the Petroleum Oil and Gas Corporation of South Africa (PetroSA), building on his previous experience in production and planning engineering with blue-chip diversified multinationals like BHP Billiton, RTZ and Lonmin.
